Areti "The Master" Mastrodouka

A huge athlete with the right attitude

Areti is an athlete with numerous titles and she is in the same time teacher of boxing and kick-boxing for kids and adults. She was the first greek woman to conquer the world boxing championship!

Here's a timeline of Areti's life:

  • 1970 - Born in Athens, Greece
  • 1978 - Starts to practice Martial Arts
  • 1985 - Achieves black belt
  • 1990 - Wins the National Boxing belt
  • 2000 - Becomes International Boxing Champion
